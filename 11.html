Create an environment

Boyfriend Material
Mar 11, 2025

"I am the master of my fate, I am the captain of my soul."

William Ernst Henley, Invictus

Do you know this poem, at least this quote? I just love it... Whenever I feel in an unconfortable position or thinking about life, decisions and all of that... Well this one gets me on the mindset that I can do anything that I want. Everything depends on me.

The next couple of days I am going to show you some of my writtings from my birthday in 2023. Why is so important? Well, that birthday was the first one that I spend completly alone. It was kind of depressing actually, and it was a red alarm for me.

That birthday I took the night train to Utrecht, so I arrived in the morning of my birthday, the 6th of April. Checked In and went to the city to do some sightseeing and of course took some photos... The highlight of the day, actually my celebration was to go to a Restaurant, got a Burger and some beer, it was delicious. After that I went to the movies to watch the new film of Keanu Reeves - "John Wick 4".

It was not that bad, but it didn't feel right. So when I came back to Switzerland I grabbed again my notebook and looked for some old notes and here is the result.

Create an environment

Spending my 42th birthday alone was like a wake up clal for me. If I keep on this road I will end up like this, alone, sad and gray.

I read this post from a crossfit coach, Ben Bergeron and it goes like this:


Create an environment... that is something that I need to work on and take action. Life as a consultant can be good, but there must be a balance, and now I need to take some time for my private life. Money is good, but at what point I am willing to sacrifice my health, mental and physical, just to keep achieving things, more money, for what?

Life past really fast, on any given time we can be gone, that is why it is better to enjoy it instead of being focus and achieve more and more in vain.

Is not conformism, I have always been against it, it is about expand my areas of knowledge, optimize my time to be able to live a healthier life. Setting aside time from work mean more time for some of my projects:

  • Photography: create more photography, have a group of people with the same interest and discover our inner voice and our way of express ourselves
  • Writting: create my book or my books with all the ideas that I have, photography bounded with music, motivation books to develop as a person (eventhough I still need to work a lot on that area, but it could be a good way to improve myself) and some toher topics, maybe a novel about life, love and redemption.
  • Healthy life: exercise regularly like when I was in Bern, Crossfit 4-5 times a week, eat healthy, cook rice, chicken and brocolli... the whole package.
    Sleep more, have a less stressful life, stress is killing me slowly, like the Radiohead song: "A job that slowly kills you..." not from boredom but from stress, I took it to the other extrem...
  • Personal Relationships: Work on my personal relationships, create friendships, have friends to enjoy a beer, a grill, watch a game. Create an emotional support circle.
  • Enjoy life: enjoy again travelling, go to restaurants, have a beer in some bar, sit on a park and just read.
  • Learn new things: learn more about art, history, literature, read more books from other topics which are no technical or self-development / improvement.

I need to change my life style, need to find the balance, need to take action.

Thinking about you... Un beso Laura
