Slow Mornings, Coffee and Thoughts - Part 1

Carlos

Today the Mexican Version
Mar 21, 2025

This is part 1 of today's blog... at this moment you have already received a video from me... which is part 2... I hope you like it.

Today I went to this company event, it was really cool and got to meet a lot of people, also i had maybe one extra beer... and this got out the Mexican inside of me...

Now i am writing you this lines, having nice thoughts about you , that I hope they don't disturb you or make you uncomfortable, it's just me thinking about you on a slow Sunday morning...I have many versions of mornings with you, that i could tell you later about them or even better really spend them with you , but for now this one seems appropriate... i hope it is ok for you...

I would love to have slow Sundays with you.
Waking up next to you, seeing how the sunlight comes inside your bedroom and illuminates a part of your hair, then i give you a kiss and notice that you are still a little sleepy... and without further thought , I try to get up quietly so as not to wake you up, go to the kitchen, take the Bialetti from your stove, look for the coffee and start the ritual of making coffee ...

After a few minutes and with the smell of freshly brewed coffee, I hear your voice from the bedroom softly telling me if I could make you a cup of coffee... The coffee is ready, I pour it in a small cup, add some milk to it and a small spoonful of sugar, just the way you like it, and so with your sleeping clothes you get up, go to the kitchen, which smells of freshly brewed coffee and slow Sunday mornings , you look at me, give me a hug full of tenderness, I kiss you on the forehead and we sit down to have the first coffee of the morning...

The small things in life…
