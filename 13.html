Nostalgia - Cinema Paradiso

Carlos

Candidate for a Great Relationship
Mar 13, 2025

"Don't come back. Don't give in to nostalgia. Forget us all. If you do and you come back, don't come see me. I won't let you in my house."

Alfredo, Cinema Paradiso

First of all, the quote, is not meant literally for you. Comeback please, I do will let you in my house.

This one is a short one, and is like the second part of the Immigrant post from some days ago

As you already read, this one is from Cinema Paradiso. I know you haven't seen it, so I won't spoil it (not too much).
There is a scene where an old person is telling this younger person to go out there and fulfill his dreams and to do that he must not give to nostalgia.

There have been many times, where I almost give up to it.Take the first plane and leave everything as it is. It hasn't been easy, but with the time you get used to it, to the memories that you have, and the memories that you are missing back with your people.

But then, you remember why you are doing this, you look at the memories you are creating here and you get the strength to keep on going and save some more for further difficult times... and every time it gets easier.
Of course, there will come a moment, when the big pile of nostalgia will knock at my door again, and this time with so much strength, that the walls will crumble and fall down... But until this moment comes, the motto still is

Dont' give in to Nostalgia...

Have a good travel to Koh Tao!
