Unknown Paths

Carlos

Candidate for a Great Relationship
Mar 07, 2025

"Andábamos sin buscarnos, pero sabiendo que andábamos para encontrarnos."

"We were walking without looking for each other, but knowing that we were walking to find each other."

Julio Cortazar, Rayuela

Destiny... I will talk a bit more about it in some other blog, but right now this quote matches a lot of things about what is going on in my head and about what is happening to us.

It is somehow crazy, in a beautiful way, how we got to met. How from almost nowhere... now we are here, at this point. Writting you my thoughts like 9,051 km apart, but hopping for you to get to know me a little bit better.

Like I told you the other day, I am kind of an emotional guy, and I don't mind to tell it loud. I like to express my feelings with the right persons... And if you haven't noticed it yet, I count you as one of this right kind of persons.

You might ask yourself why? Why me? Well, I don't know, it's just a feeling. It's the energy that I get from you, the peace that I feel when I am with you, the happines that struck me the moment I see you on the street or at the train station when we meet or the feeling (or hunch) that you are a good person with a good heart... Maybe the calmness of your voice or the elegance of your movements... I think is all of it, and more things that I can't explain.

Anyway, I am truly happy we got to meet and that we are at this point, thinking about spending nice spring days together or having special summer evenings sharing thoughts, cuddles, laughs, flirtatious glances and lovely moments, the ones that build memories.

Enjoy your first day in Thailand!
