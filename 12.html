Today is a short Post I think, but I hope is somehow interesting
So this one is still about 2023, with my existencial doubts and all of it. Writting these lines right now make me realize how far I've become and that I've grown stronger in my mind and in my soul. It has been an interesting ride.

Looking for answers when you already have them

This is some collage of my thoughts at the moment:

Blink - A book from Malcolm Gladwell talks about how humans take decisions, the book is very interesting, it has some stories backed up by science. Anyway, everything is about intuition, our first instinct, that first response , that first thought is mostly the right answer.

We ourselves already now the answers to those questions that somehow we struggle deciding what is the right thing to do.

When I came back from Mexico that year, I had a lot of plans without sense, well that moment they didn't make sense. After some time they started to make sense.

Some of the plans were the following

January: Applied for a Job in Spain, so I wanted to fly and see the city (Sevilla) -> Anyway I did it one year later 2024

February: Going to a Champions League game in Milan -> that I actually did it in April
I wanted to go to Skandinavia, go to a cottage, in the woods, in winter, in Finnland -> To do

March: Surf in Sri Lanka
Take my parents to Tokio for my birthday (Option 1)

April: Got to the Masters, a golf Tournament with my parents for my birthday (Option 2)
Have some friends for visit in Lugano -> they did went to visit me

May: Mom comes to visit me -> she did
Quit my Job -> Actually I did it

Actually some of the things that I've planned I did it, some of them took some time, some of them I don't know if I am going to make them, but what is important is to have some ideas, projects, and goals.

With the time I realized that my gut feeling, the sixth sense in me is not that bad at guessing and taking decisions. Maybe for that reason I think that we can become a "we" , I have a good feeling about it, something inside me tells me that... let's see

Now I have a question for you. It is not about decisions, or Blink or destiny...
It is still about a book though.

Atomic habits, you are reading it. So my question is which habits do you have that you like to keep doing and which habits would you like to have or improve?
