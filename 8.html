The perfection trap

Carlos

Aspiring Partner for Finnish - Swiss girl
Mar 08, 2025

"And now that you don't have to be perfect you can be good."

John Steinbeck

I love this quote. It is so liberating and full of hope. At some point in life we should start putting all of our energies into being good. Not because we didn't try it before, but because at some point we realize, that perfection doesn't exist. And while we are looking for perfection, we miss a lot of beautiful things, moments and persons.
For some people, this awakening moment happens earlier than for some others. For some people it just comes to late in life.

For me, it came after a break up, followed by depression. But it was in this depressive phase, when I reached this point.

So, what I am trying to say is, that from some bad moments, something good came. I grew and found some peace allong the road.
