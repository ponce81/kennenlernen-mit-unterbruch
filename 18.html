Carlos

Candidate for a Great Relationship
Mar 18, 2025

"If you want to go fast, go alone. If you want to go far, go with others."

African proverb

This one is very interesting and very true. Until now I have been focusing on going fast. But in the last years, I realize I don't want to do that. I want to go far. So I am looking for my team partner, the one to walk together through life and everything.

If everything goes well, like the way it has been going with us, and as I expect it to be, I hope in some months I could say that I found the team which I would like to belong (and hopefully you as well of course).

But please don't freak out about these lines, we have a long road full of excitement, with different stages that we will go through, building step by step at its own time... but the main idea remains... if you want to go far, you need a compass (kind of like the north star for the sailors) - and these lines are something like that.

Looking forward to have many conversations with you about this, with a glass of wine at the balcony or laying on the grass in a park.

Keyword is the book Designing your life... I think you will like it.

Enjoy your day!
