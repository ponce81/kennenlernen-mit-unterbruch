This thing called destiny

Carlos

Aspiring Partner for Finnish - Swiss girl
Mar 09, 2025

"Let life happen and bring what it has to bring. Believe me, life is always, always right."

Rainer Maria Rilke

I've always believed that destiny plays a role in our lives. It has something poetic and nostalgic at the same time. Someone put us here in this earth, in this time and under these conditions. Gave us some tools like Intelligence and physical qualities... What we do with these resources under these conditions is entirely up to us.

And this is something that has marked me since I was a teenager. Decisions... Now the tree theory... Life is like a tree, we start on the roots, which symbolizes our family. We grew up in a more or less (hopefully) safe environment and at some point we started to be confronted with decisions. This is where the stem, the trunk starts to diverge. With each decision a new branch opens up to us. And to comeback to a past state is dificult almost impossible. That's why decisions are important.

So, which have been my most life changing decisions that I had to make?

  • Quit football to become an Engineer - Age 18 years old
  • Study in Germany (and stay here in Europe) - Age 25 years old
  • Ending some major relationships

How do I feel about my biggest decisions so far? ... Well... most of the times is "No regrets", they've made me the person that I am, and eventhough I am not nearly close to perfection (and don't want to be) I am happy most of the times with the person that I am.

But some other times I ask myself a lot of things, the what ifs... Let me start with an example... Football. I started playing when I was 7 years old, I wasn't that good, but I trained long and hard, and when I was 15 I was both in the school first team and in the young team of one of the most important teams in Mexico. At the age of 16 I signed my first contract as a football player, started travelling and playing in stadiums. Wherever we went, people ask us for our autograph, it was crazy and I was living the dream.

Then I turned 18 and finished high school, and I knew I had to make a decision. I was good with my head, studies. And I also was good playing football, I knew I could make it to the first team and maybe national.
And I choose study and become an engineer.

While I was in the university the guy who took my place in the team got called to the first team as a professional, big thing. And then he got better and better at the point that he played 3 World cups for the Mexican National Team.
Deep down inside me, I had to prove myself that I could be as succesful in the studies as I could have been as a football player. I don't know if that was the right mindset, but it became my engine, my motivation and put my heart and soul on studies and work.

I put aside a lot of times being young and happy... and trade it for trying to be successfull. One thing that I know is that because of those times that I stayed at home learning and working on some projects, those times are the reason that I am here, life got me to this point and that, I don't regret it

Now the question that I have had the last couple of years is when is enough, until which point I have to sacrifice some things for success, and what is success... What I know is, that it is time for me to focus my effort in my private life... Being successfull might be to be happy, and I want to be happy and I am working on that.


Today while I was writting my learnings from past relationships I took this notebook, which sometimes use to just put my thoughts on paper, in order. And I found some interesting pages. One of this is the following. I wrote it somewhere between March 2023 and July 2023 while I was living in Lugano.

It might sound a bit depressing, but is more somehow like a philosophical retoric speech with myself

Notes about decisions

In a room, watching, meditating, he realizes that his life was not what he had in mind how it would be. He doesn't know how he got to this point, but in that instant his mind travels to that point of inflexion, that moment when his life could have been different in case he had taken some other decision, taken the other path.

In life we had a lot of inflexion points, the tree theory, the branches until reach the leafs.

What is better? To reach the top and see the world from that perspective?

Or stay in the middle where the fruits grow, where the flowers bloom...


For everyone there is a different story , during a whole life a lot fo different stories.

In a room with a beautiful view to the alps, surrounded by all these luxuries which never in his life thought he could have , and never look for, in that moment he realizes ( or asked himself) that is not the place where he wanted to be, not the life that he imagined he would have.

Where is this boy with happy eyes and big smile (with his big teeth). Who dreamed about changing the world or have a positive impact in society. Is that what he wanted? Or he just only wanted to be happy...

I only want to be happy...

I have grown a lot the last couple of years, I know what my priorities are... being happy and enjoy life. And I have been doing it, and that got me to the point to focus in my private life. Family, friends and now hopefully you, a partner... I say that bluntly and direct...

You know... these thoughts and points of views not a lot of people now about them, I might told some of this to my mom, but not all of them. Even my best friends doesn't know about these thoughts and feelings. These pages are private, are my private notes. And here I am, being vulnerable to you. I don't know how and why but I trust you with these private thoughts. It is me, opening myself to you, letting my guard down, hoping for the best. Taking a leap of faith.

Have a good travel and enjoy the time with your favorite girls...

Besos
